Training Process

A compact map of the current OD LeWM path from simulator data to the loss terms being optimized.

Orekit propagationHidden ECI state [r, v] evolves with the Eckstein-Hechler J2-J6 analytical propagator.
Ground station viewThe learner sees range, azimuth, elevation, and range-rate with configured measurement noise.
Windowed datasetShort normalized windows pair observations with zero LVLH acceleration actions.
EncoderA vector MLP maps each 4D observation into a 192D LeWM embedding.
AR predictorA conditional transformer predicts future embeddings from context and action embeddings.
LossesPrediction MSE pulls next-step embeddings together; SIGReg keeps the latent space well spread.
